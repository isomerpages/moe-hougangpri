<p>Dear Parents and Guardian</p>
<p>You are warmly invited to MOE Kindergarten @ Hougang Open House on Saturday
7 March 2026. This is a day where you can tour the kindergarten classrooms
and fun outdoor spaces. You can also speak to our teachers to understand
more about what we teach and how we make learning fun and purposeful. Our
kindergarten is a happy place where <strong>“Play-full Learning Today” leads to Purpose-full Learners Tomorrow”.</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Event Details:</strong>
</p>
<p>Date : Saturday 7 March 2026</p>
<p>Time : 9am to 12pm</p>
<p>Venue : MOE Kindergarten @ Hougang</p>
<p></p>
<p>Come and find out how MOE Kindergarten prepares your child for a “Strong
Start” in learning and also instil values of care, respect, responsibility
and integrity in our unique programme.</p>
