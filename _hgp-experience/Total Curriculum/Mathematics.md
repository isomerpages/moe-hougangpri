<p><strong>Problem Solving<br></strong>Knowing how to solve problems is an
important skill and an essential part of our lives. We need to spend considerable
effort and time on describing how problem solving should be taught and
learned.</p>
<p><strong>General Approach</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 25%;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/math4.jpg">
</div>
<p><strong>Understand the Problem</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Read the problem carefully to understand what is required</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Break the problem up into smaller parts and understand each part</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Visualise the information</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Organise the information</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Connect the information</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Draw/Write the information into simpler form</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Plan What to do / Devise a Plan</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Choose an appropriate heuristic / method to solve the problem</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Do / Carry out the Plan</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Use computational skills ( +, -, ร and รท ), geometrical skills and logical
reasoning to carry out plan</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Check the Solution</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Check the reasonableness of the solution and answer</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Improve / Change method used</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Seek alternative solutions</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Extend method to other problems</p>
</li>
</ul>
